Our Story

Supporting Researchers Since Day One

CRHLS – Center for Research Help and Learning Support – was founded with a clear purpose: to break down the barriers that prevent students, researchers, and professionals from conducting meaningful research.

Research is a critical skill for academic and professional development. Yet many individuals face significant challenges with methodology, survey design, statistical analysis, software tools, and academic formatting. CRHLS was built to simplify this journey.

Through structured guidance, expert mentorship, and practical training, we have supported hundreds of learners in achieving their research goals — from undergraduate dissertations to professional feasibility studies.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To support students and professionals in developing strong research and analytical skills through mentorship, training, and practical guidance — making research more accessible, understandable, and efficient for learners at every level.

🌟

Our Vision

To become the leading research support and learning center in Bangladesh, where every student, researcher, and professional can confidently access the guidance they need to produce impactful, high-quality research.

🤝

Our Approach

We combine academic rigor with practical mentorship. Every session is tailored to the individual learner's topic, level, and goals — ensuring that support is always relevant, clear, and immediately actionable.

📈

Our Impact

From helping a first-year student understand sampling techniques to supporting a professional researcher in publishing journal articles — CRHLS creates measurable, lasting impact in the lives of those we serve.
