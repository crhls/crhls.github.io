Service 01

Research Mentorship & Guidance

Navigating the research process can feel overwhelming — especially for first-time researchers. Our mentors provide structured, step-by-step guidance from topic selection through to final submission.

Whether you are working on a thesis, dissertation, journal paper, or professional report, our mentors will walk alongside you, clarifying concepts, reviewing your progress, and keeping you on track.

  • Topic selection and scope definition
  • Literature review strategy and structure
  • Research design and methodology planning
  • Progress review and milestone support

Service 02

Survey & Questionnaire Design

A poorly designed survey can undermine even the most carefully conceived research. Our experts guide you in developing valid, reliable instruments that gather the right data from the right respondents.

We cover question types, Likert scales, skip logic, response options, and pilot testing — ensuring your instrument is clear, unbiased, and academically robust.

  • Questionnaire structure and flow
  • Validated scale selection (Likert, Guttman, etc.)
  • Online survey platform setup (Google Forms, SurveyMonkey)
  • Pilot testing and refinement
📊

Statistics & Data Analysis Training

We train you to understand and apply statistical concepts — from descriptive statistics and hypothesis testing to regression, ANOVA, and factor analysis — in your research context.

  • Descriptive & inferential statistics
  • Hypothesis testing & p-values
  • Regression & correlation analysis
  • Non-parametric tests
💻

Research Software Training

Hands-on, practical training in the most widely used research and analysis tools. Learn to perform real analyses, interpret outputs, and produce publication-ready results.

  • SPSS – surveys & social science
  • STATA – econometrics & health research
  • R – statistical computing & visualization
  • Python & Excel for data analysis
🔍

Analysis & Interpretation Support

Having data is one thing — making sense of it is another. We guide you in reading, interpreting, and presenting your findings clearly and in alignment with your research objectives.

  • Output reading & interpretation
  • Tables, charts & figure preparation
  • Discussion & conclusion writing
  • Linking findings to the literature
📄

Documentation & Formatting Support

Professional presentation of your research is essential. We assist with structure, layout, citation management, and formatting to meet the standards of your institution or publication.

  • APA, MLA & Chicago style formatting
  • Reference management (Mendeley, Zotero)
  • Thesis/dissertation structuring
  • Report & academic paper formatting
