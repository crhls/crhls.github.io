Who We Are

Simplifying the Research Process

Research is an important skill for academic and professional development. However, many individuals face challenges with research methodology, survey design, statistical analysis, software tools, and academic formatting.

CRHLS (Center for Research Help and Learning Support) aims to simplify this process by offering structured guidance, mentorship, and practical learning support.

Our goal is to support students and professionals in developing strong research and analytical skills. We focus on making research accessible, understandable, and efficient for learners.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Research Services

Mentorship & Guidance

Step-by-step guidance to help individuals understand the research process and conduct their studies more effectively.

Survey & Questionnaire

Guidance on developing well-structured surveys and questionnaires using appropriate research methods.

Statistics Training

Training and support to understand statistical concepts and apply them in research data analysis.

Software Training

Practical learning support for SPSS, STATA, R, Python, and Microsoft Excel.

Analysis & Interpretation

Guidance in understanding research findings and presenting results in a clear and meaningful way.

Documentation Support

Assistance in organizing and formatting research documents according to standard academic practices.
